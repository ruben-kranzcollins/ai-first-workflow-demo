## Which screenshot matters most?

**"Pull request blocked before approval"** is the key screenshot.

It proves that the workflow is not just an agreement on paper: GitHub itself blocks the merge until a human has reviewed and approved the change. That single image answers the main management question — *"how do we know AI output is checked before it lands?"*

Second most important is the **filled PR template** (AI use + safety check visible), because it shows that AI use is disclosed and sensitive data is explicitly checked on every change.
